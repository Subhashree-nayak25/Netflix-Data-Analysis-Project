### 🎬 **Netflix Data Analysis**



#### 📌 **Project Overview**

#### **📊 Key Findings**

###### 

###### **Q1. What is the most frequent genre?**

###### 

###### **Drama** is the most frequent genre, accounting for more than **14%** of all movies.

###### 

###### **Q2. Which genre has the highest votes?**

###### 

###### **Drama** also received the highest number of votes, representing more than **18.5%** of highly voted movies.

###### 

###### **Q3. Which movie has the highest popularity?**

###### 

###### **Spider-Man: No Way Home** has the highest popularity in the dataset and belongs to the **Action, Adventure, and Science Fiction** genres.

###### 

###### **Q4. Which movie has the lowest popularity?**

###### 

###### **The United States, Thread** has the lowest popularity and belongs to the **Music, Drama, War, Sci-Fi, and History** genres.

###### 

###### **Q5. Which year has the highest number of movie releases?**

###### 

###### **2020** recorded the highest number of movie releases in the dataset.
